About Me

Born amongst wolves: forged in smoke and fire; Adam Wolcott Smith is a baroque, acid-rock keyboardist as well as a trained power-pop guitar thespian. From and inspired by the ever pastoral central Virginian landscape, but now residing in Brooklyn, NY, he writes to shoothe as and agitate. For fans of the Kinks, XTC, Squeeze, Wire, Buzzcocks, Sparks and snacks of all kinds. He's been doing this since he was a wee lad, but also has found interest in other pursuits such as web development and cooking.
